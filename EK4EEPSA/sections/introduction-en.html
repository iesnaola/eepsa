Introduction back to ToC

The EK4EEPSA (Expert Knowledge ontology module for the EEPSA Ontology) captures the necessary knowledge to provide inferencing capabilities that can be exploited by data analysts in the KDD Data Selection phase. Towards such a goal, a group of thermal and energy domain experts were interviewed to elicit and formalise their knowledge, and capture it in the ontology module in a proper way.
